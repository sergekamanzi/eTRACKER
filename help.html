Help & Support

Welcome to the eTracker help section. Here, you can find information and assistance on how to use various features of the eTracker application.

Getting Started

To start using eTracker, you need to create an account. Once registered, you can log in and start tracking your expenses.

  • Create Account: Fill in your details on the sign-up page and submit the form.
  • Log In: Enter your credentials on the login page and access your dashboard.

Dashboard Overview

The dashboard provides an overview of your financial status, including your total income, expenses, and savings. You can also view recent transactions and charts that help visualize your spending patterns.

Managing Reports

In the Reports section, you can generate detailed reports on your financial activities. This includes monthly expense breakdowns, historical data analysis, and comparison charts to track your progress over time.

  • View Reports: Navigate to the Reports section from the sidebar and select the type of report you want to view.
  • Export Reports: You can export reports in various formats such as PDF, Excel, or CSV for further analysis or record-keeping.

Settings

In the Settings section, you can customize your eTracker experience:

  • Profile Settings: Update your personal information and preferences.
  • Notification Settings: Configure how and when you receive notifications about your financial activities.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I reset my password? Go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password." Follow the instructions to reset your password.
  • Can I add multiple accounts? Yes, you can add multiple bank accounts and track expenses for each separately.
  • Is my data secure? Yes, we use advanced security measures to ensure your data is protected and confidential.
